## 1. Circular Agriculture & Agribusiness

| Idea | One-liner | Metric | Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **SakaFeed** | Least-cost feed ration from local byproducts (rice bran, copra, market waste; banana optional ingredient not brand) | ₱ feed saved / week | ⭐ 🧪 | Best non-banana agri default |

| **KompostKita** | Barangay compost credit loop (QR weigh-in → credits) | tons composted / barangay / mo | Stretch | Needs co-op story |
| **AbonoKita** | Fertilizer vs compost / organic mix decision for smallholders | ₱ input saved | Bench | Can feel generic without Tagum prices |
| **PresyoLuto (veg spoilage)** | Public-market vegetable spoilage clock (FreshTrack for produce) | ₱ spoilage avoided | Bench | Overlaps FreshTrack shape |
| **BalotVerde** (agri-adjacent) | Compostable packaging supply link (leaf wraps, fiber trays) | plastics displaced / mo | Stretch | Pairs with banana chain; stretch |
